# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in contributing to Sentinel Agentic OS.

## License of contributions & the CLA

This project is licensed under the **GNU AGPLv3** (see [`LICENSE.md`](LICENSE.md)).

To keep the project's licensing options open — including offering a separate
commercial edition and the ability to evolve the license in the future — **all
contributors must sign a Contributor License Agreement (CLA)** before their
contribution can be merged. The CLA grants the maintainer the rights needed to
license your contribution both under the AGPLv3 and under the project's commercial
terms; **you retain copyright in your contribution.**

When you open your first pull request, an automated CLA check (CLA Assistant) will
ask you to sign. PRs cannot be merged until the CLA is signed.

> Note: a commercial edition of this software is developed separately and is **not**
> a derivative of this AGPL codebase. The CLA exists so the project itself remains
> relicensable, not to absorb your work into the closed edition without the rights
> you granted.

## How to contribute

1. Open an issue describing the bug or proposal before large changes, so we can
   agree on the approach.
2. Fork, branch, and keep changes focused and small.
3. Match the surrounding code style; the frontend is dependency-free vanilla JS and
   the backend is FastAPI — no build step.
4. Add or update tests where they apply, and make sure the suite passes.
5. Open a PR with a clear description; sign the CLA when prompted.

## Code of conduct

Be respectful and constructive. Harassment or abuse of any kind is not tolerated.

## Reporting security issues

Please **do not** open public issues for security vulnerabilities. Email
**catalin.adrian.tudor@gmail.com** with details and allow reasonable time for a fix
before any disclosure.
